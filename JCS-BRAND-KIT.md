# JCS — Brand Kit Handoff

A complete brief for designing branded assets for **JCS (Jacob Schrader)**.
Paste this at the start of any Claude design session. Everything below is
taken from the live site (jacobcschrader.com) — match it exactly; do not
invent new colors, fonts, or taglines.

---

## 1. The brand in one paragraph

JCS is the personal, premium brand of Jacob Schrader — real estate,
architecture & design **photography + film** for luxury listings. It is the
top tier above his volume company (The Visaro): a select number of projects
each year, shot personally, to the highest standard. Positioning line:
**"Built for the 1%."** The brand should feel like the homes it shoots:
calm, editorial, expensive, unhurried. Think Sotheby's catalog, not
Instagram ad.

- Tagline / descriptor: **Real Estate · Architecture · Design**
- Sub-line used on site: *"Based in Danville, CA — available worldwide."*
- Clients: Serhant, Compass, Sotheby's, Coldwell Banker, Berkshire Hathaway,
  Engel & Völkers, Keller Williams, Casa Tua
- Press: The Wall Street Journal, Robb Report, Tahoe Quarterly, The Bee, SF Gate

## 2. Wordmark / monogram

The logo IS typography — no icon, no symbol, no camera imagery ever.

- Monogram: **"JCS"** set in **Cormorant Garamond, italic, weight 300**,
  letter-spacing ~0.01em. This exact treatment is the header brand and
  footer wordmark on the site.
- Full name lockup (secondary): "Jacob Schrader" in Cormorant Garamond,
  with "Real Estate · Architecture · Design" beneath in Inter, small caps
  or uppercase, wide letter-spacing (0.15–0.2em).
- Never bold the monogram. Never add a period after JCS. Never outline,
  bevel, gradient, or shadow it.

## 3. Color palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Navy (primary) | `#0f2240` | The brand color. Dark sections, monogram on light, buttons |
| Navy deep | `#091627` | Near-black backdrop, video placeholders |
| Navy soft | `#1c3252` | Hover states, secondary navy |
| Ink | `#16233b` | Body text on white |
| Paper | `#ffffff` | Primary background |
| Paper warm | `#f6f4ef` | Warm off-white sections, subtle contrast |
| Muted | `#5d6a7e` | Captions, metadata, small labels |
| White on dark | `rgba(255,255,255,0.7)` | Secondary text on navy |

Rules: white/navy is the identity — high whitespace, no accent colors, no
gradients. Black (`#000`) appears only as photo overlay scrims, never as a
design surface.

## 4. Typography

- **Display / headings:** Cormorant Garamond (weights 300–600, italic for
  emphasis and the monogram). Headlines often set in italic sentence case:
  e.g. *"Cinematic Filmmaking"*, *"Selected projects."*
- **Body / UI:** Inter (300–600). Small labels are uppercase with wide
  tracking (0.15–0.2em) — the "eyebrow" style: e.g. `FILMS`, `RECOGNITION`.
- Google Fonts: `Cormorant+Garamond:ital,wght@0,300;0,400;0,500;0,600;1,400`
  and `Inter:wght@300;400;500;600`.
- Pairing rule: serif carries elegance, sans carries information. Never use
  the serif for paragraphs of body text; never use Inter for display lines.

## 5. Voice

Calm, confident, spare. Short declarative sentences. Quiet faith undertone
is welcome ("blessed," "calling") but never preachy. No exclamation points,
no hype words ("stunning!", "amazing"). Signature copy for reference:

> "Two years ago, what began as a hobby became a calling. Since then, I've
> been blessed to travel the country photographing and filming some of the
> nation's most spectacular homes — more than $5 billion in property, and
> counting."
>
> "JCS is where I take that work furthest. Built for the 1% — a select
> number of projects each year, shot personally, to the highest standard."

## 6. Design principles

1. Photography is the hero — layouts exist to frame images, not compete.
2. Generous whitespace; editorial margins; nothing crowded.
3. Text over photos gets a black scrim (gradient overlay), image at ~0.68
   opacity beneath — never colored overlays.
4. Motion is subtle: gentle fades and reveals, no parallax, no zooming heroes.
5. Thin 1px rules (`rgba(15,34,64,0.14)`) separate sections — never boxes
   or cards with heavy shadows.

## 7. Assets to produce (the kit)

1. **Logo suite:** JCS monogram + Jacob Schrader lockup, each in navy-on-white,
   white-on-navy, and white-on-photo versions. SVG + transparent PNG (1x/2x/3x).
2. **Watermark:** low-opacity white JCS monogram for photo/video corners.
3. **Business card:** 3.5×2", paper white front (monogram + name), navy back.
   Contact: jacxbschrader@gmail.com · (408) 824-8719 · jacobcschrader.com
4. **Email signature:** HTML, matching the site's transactional email (navy
   header band, serif italic headline, Inter details).
5. **Social templates:** Instagram post + story frames (photo full-bleed,
   monogram watermark, eyebrow-style captions); profile avatar (monogram
   white on navy).
6. **Film assets:** end-card / title-card for videos (navy, centered JCS
   monogram, thin rule, project name in Cormorant italic).
7. **Proposal/PDF cover:** letterhead-style template for quotes and shot lists.

## 8. Hard rules (never do)

- No camera icons, lens motifs, aperture symbols, or house icons.
- No colors outside §3. No gradients except black photo scrims.
- No new fonts. No bold monogram. No "JCS." with a period.
- Nothing that would look at home on a volume/discount brand — when in
  doubt, remove elements rather than add.
