The short version

Raised in the Bay Area and educated at Grand Canyon University, I built my career in media and design — founding The Visaro, a real estate media company I still lead, serving agents and listings at scale.

Two years ago, what began as a hobby became a calling. Since then, I've been blessed to travel the country photographing and filming some of the nation's most spectacular homes — more than $5 billion in property, and counting.

JCS is where I take that work furthest. Built for the 1% — a select number of projects each year, shot personally, without compromise.

Based in Diablo, CA — serving worldwide.
