The short version

A decade spent learning how buildings hold light.

I came to architecture through photography, and to film through architecture. What began as a fascination with how a single window changes a room has become a full practice — making still and moving images for agents, architects, developers, and designers.

My approach is unhurried. I'd rather wait an hour for the right light than force a frame, and I'd rather understand a building before I photograph it. The work that results is calm, deliberate, and made to sell.

Based in Diablo, CA — serving worldwide.
